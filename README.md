## About

This repository hosts an interactive web presentation of the scientific article **"Dark Matter as Gravitational Trace of Time Annihilation"** by Sheglakov Aleksandr.

The article proposes a novel theoretical framework that connects the phenomenon of dark matter to fundamental aspects of temporal physics, suggesting that what we observe as dark matter may be a gravitational signature of time annihilation processes at cosmological scales.

## ✨ Features

- **Black hole animation** — immersive landing page with a CSS gravitational lensing effect
- **Bilingual** — full article available in English and Russian
- **Downloadable** — get the article as HTML or PDF in either language
- **Responsive** — works on desktop, tablet, and mobile
- **PWA** — install as an app with offline support
- **Contacts** — author contact information and social links

1. Open the [live article](https://haytako.github.io/dark-matter-article/) on your phone
