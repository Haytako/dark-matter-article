About This Project

This website presents the sixth edition of a scientific hypothesis proposing that dark matter consists of topological defects of spacetime — “knots of curvature” — left behind by unclosed time loops during particle-antiparticle annihilation in the early Universe. The document was developed through a multi-party discussion with AI models (z.ai, DeepSeek, Kimi, Alice) and includes mathematical calculations, observational predictions, and independent reviews.
